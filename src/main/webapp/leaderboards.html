Rules

  • Initiating a Game: Choose to play against other players or challenge the computer.
  • Online Play: Generate a unique code that allows both users to join the same game when entered.
  • Objective: The primary objective of this game is to capture your opponent's king.
  • Winning by Timer: You can win by depleting your opponent's time; when they run out of time, you win.
  • Piece Capture: Capturing an opponent's piece is achieved by dragging your piece onto theirs.
  • Special Moves: Castling, en passant, and pawn promotion are special moves in chess.
  • Stalemate: Stalemate occurs when a player has no legal moves.
